(2) SABJI RAJMAHAL
(Fresh Vegetables cooked in an aromatic roasted coriander seeds curry)
This dish is very popular in the northern part of India. The base of this dish is prepared with vine ripped tomatoes, coriander seeds and a mixture of many more spices and then the fresh vegetables like carrots, eggplants,basil,corns,red creamer, blue lake beans, cauliflower and many other are added and cooked.
$5.00
(3) CHETTIAR CHICKEN
(Boneless chicken cooked in ginger, black pepper and curry leaves curry)
This dis comes from the south Indian community. They use the strict traditional methods to prepare this dish. First the cubes of chicken are marinated in the mix of turmeric and ginger paste and are kept aside for some time. The base is prepared with roasted lentil powder, fennel seeds, mustard seeds, garlic, red onions, and vine ripened tomatoes. The marinated chicken is added to the base gravy.
